When given a matrix M that represents a linear transformation, to figure out an enlargement you must square root the determinant of M
$$
\begin{gather*}
\det M=(1)(1)-(\sqrt{ -3 })(\sqrt{ 3 }) = 4\\
k = \sqrt{ 4 } = 2
\end{gather*}
$$
